How it works

Sit Daily is intentionally simple. A Session Steward is present for timing and technical support — not instruction. You arrive, you sit, you leave.

1) Choose a time

Pick a session that fits your day. Consistency beats intensity.

2) Join the room

Mic off by default. Camera optional. We start exactly on time.

3) Sit for 20 minutes

Guided + music, music only, or silent timer — you choose.

Session formats

Choose your style without explanation. No one is asked to share. No one is evaluated.

Guided + music

Minimal guidance paired with calm audio. Best if you want a gentle structure.

Music only

Steady soundscape, no voice. For people who want presence without narration.

Silent timer

Pure practice. A simple timer holds the container while you do your own method.

Quiet by design

No discussion before/after. This keeps the space professional and low-friction.

FAQ

Do I have to turn my camera on?

No. Camera is optional. This is designed to be low-pressure.

Do people talk or share?

No. The room is intentionally quiet. We don’t do check-ins, reflections, or discussions.

Is this guided meditation?

Sometimes (minimally). But the core value is the daily container and shared presence, not content.

What if I miss a day?

Just return the next day. No guilt, no restart, no “streak culture.”

Who is this for?

People who value structure and want meditation to become daily—especially those with busy lives and high cognitive load.
